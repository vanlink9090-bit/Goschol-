Refund Policy

Effective date: 21 July 2026  ·  Applies to Goschol subscription payments

Note on this document: this policy covers subscription payments made via UPI directly to the developer's UPI ID. Have it reviewed by someone qualified before relying on it for a public launch, and update the refund window or examples below if your process changes.
1. Refund window 2. When a refund will be given 3. When a refund will not be given 4. How to request a refund 5. Our discretion 6. Contact us

01 Refund window

Refund requests must be made within 3 days of the payment date. Requests made after this window will not be eligible for a refund, regardless of the reason.

02 When a refund will be given

Refunds are given only where there is a genuine problem on our end — not simply because you changed your mind. Examples of a genuine problem:

  • You were charged but never got access — payment approved on your end (or stuck pending for an unreasonably long time) but the app never unlocked.
  • You were charged twice for the same subscription period (duplicate/accidental double payment).
  • You were charged the wrong amount (more than the listed price) due to a technical error.
  • A genuine app bug or bug in our payment verification process prevented you from using the service you paid for.

03 When a refund will NOT be given

  • You simply changed your mind or no longer want the subscription.
  • You already used the app's paid features for a meaningful part of the period.
  • Your account was suspended or access was removed for violating the Terms & Conditions.
  • The request is made more than 3 days after the payment date.
  • You provided an incorrect UPI transaction ID or an unreadable screenshot, and didn't respond to a request to resubmit.

04 How to request a refund

Use the "Refund / Issue" button on the payment screen in the app, or contact us at the email below, within 3 days of payment. Include your transaction ID and a short description of the problem. We'll review and respond, and any approved refund will be processed back to the original payment method.

05 Our discretion

Every request is reviewed individually. We reserve the right to decline a refund request that falls outside this policy, and to approve a refund in good-faith edge cases not explicitly listed above.
