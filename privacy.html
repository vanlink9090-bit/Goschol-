Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 July 2026  ·  Applies to the Goschol Android app

Note on this document: Goschol's app did not previously have a dedicated Privacy Policy — only a Terms & Conditions screen that briefly touched on data use. This page is a complete Privacy Policy written to cover what the app actually collects (including student and driver details, and live location while a driver has sharing turned on), and it should replace any placeholder text still in the app itself.

01 Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to anyone using Goschol as a Driver or a Parent/Guardian. Goschol is designed for adults to coordinate school transport — students themselves do not create accounts or use the app directly, but their name and route details are entered into it by a parent or driver, which is why Section 5 below applies specifically to that information.

02 Information we collect

We collect only what's needed to run the features described in the app:

  • Account details: name, phone number, email address, and profile photo.
  • Driver details: driving license number, vehicle registration number, school name, and school emergency contact.
  • Student details: name, class/grade, and pickup/drop-off information, entered by a parent and confirmed by a driver.
  • Messages: chat messages sent between a linked driver and parent, including quick-reply messages.
  • Live driver location: while a driver has location sharing turned on for a trip, Goschol records their GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) so the linked parent can see the van's progress. Location updates roughly every 4 seconds while sharing is active, and stop the moment the driver turns sharing off.
  • Linking data: the driver code or QR code used to connect a parent account to a driver account.
  • Device & diagnostic data: a push-notification token (to deliver chat and arrival alerts) and basic crash/error information.

A driver's live location is visible only to the parent(s) linked to that driver's route — never to other drivers, other parents, or anyone outside that connection.

03 Device permissions

  • Internet: required for all app functionality (Supabase backend).
  • Camera / Photos: used only when you choose to add or update a profile photo.
  • Phone (Call): used only when you tap a call button (driver, ambulance, police, or school) — Goschol does not place or monitor these calls itself.
  • Notifications: used to deliver chat messages and arrival alerts to your device.
  • Location (fine/coarse): used by a driver's device only while they've turned on live location sharing for a trip, and by a parent's device when picking a pickup/drop point on the map. Location access continues in the background only for as long as a driver keeps sharing turned on, shown by a persistent notification.
  • Display over other apps: powers an optional floating navigation overlay for drivers, so route directions stay visible over other apps.

You can review or withdraw any of these permissions at any time from your device's app settings; doing so may disable the related feature.

04 How we use your information

  • To link the correct driver and parent accounts together.
  • To display driver, vehicle, and student details on the relevant cards inside the app.
  • To show a parent their linked driver's live location on a map while sharing is turned on.
  • To enable direct chat between a linked driver and parent.
  • To send chat and arrival-related push notifications.
  • To cache your student list and recent messages on your device for offline access.
  • To maintain the security of accounts and investigate misuse when reported.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

05 Children's information

Goschol is not directed at children, and children do not hold accounts or interact with the app. A student's name, class, and pickup/drop details are entered by their own parent or guardian (or confirmed by the assigned driver) solely to coordinate transport. This information is treated with the same protections as any other personal data in this policy, is visible only to the linked driver and parent for that student, and is never used for advertising or shared with unrelated third parties. A parent may request deletion of their child's information at any time using the contact details in Section 12.

06 Third-party services we use

Goschol relies on the following processors to operate. Each only receives what's needed to provide its service, and none are permitted to sell your data:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage, and real-time chat delivery.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivery of push notifications (chat alerts and arrival notices) to your device.

We do not use these services for advertising, and we do not permit them to use your data for their own purposes.

07 Data storage & security

  • Data is stored with Supabase using row-level security, so drivers and parents can only access records they're directly linked to.
  • Profile and vehicle photos are stored in access-controlled storage.
  • A limited copy of your student list and recent messages is cached on your own device to support offline use.
  • No system is 100% secure; if we become aware of a breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users and take reasonable steps to limit the impact.

08 Data retention

We keep account, student, chat, and location-sharing data for as long as your account remains active and linked, so the app continues to function correctly. Live location points are only ever stored for the current trip and are overwritten on the next update — Goschol does not build a historical location trail. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where limited information must be kept to meet a legal obligation or to resolve a reported safety or misuse issue.

09 Your choices & rights

  • Access, correct, or update your profile and student information directly within the app.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Unlink from a driver or parent at any time by using the in-app "Change Driver" option or by contacting us.

To exercise any of these choices, contact us using the details in Section 12.

10 International data transfer

Our service providers may process and store data on servers located outside your own country. Where this happens, we rely on those providers' own contractual and security safeguards to protect your information to a standard consistent with this policy.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with a new effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of Goschol after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
